Extracurricular

The following are the non-academic activities and courses I joined.


- Swimming (2008-2012). I first joined a swimming course at a sports club nearby when I was 6. I used to practice various swimming strokes and techniques once a week. After 4 years, I was able to perform 5 different swimming styles at moderate speed. 


- Badminton (2014-2017). I started playing badminton twice a week with my cousins when I was 12. I stopped in 2017, due to locational issues, as the journey from my home to the badminton courts took up too much time during the week.


- Tennis (2012-2018). I started playing tennis in 2012, and I was first taught by my grandfather who used to play tennis regularly. After two years, I started regularly playing at the sports club near my home, participating in a weekly tennis course.


- Futsal (2012-2014). I participated in my school's futsal extracurricular program for two years, from 2012 to 2014. I mostly played in defense.


- Public Speaking (2015-2016). I joined a public speaking course in 2015 to improve my skills in carrying out speeches, and finding much-needed confidence when having to speak in a public setting. I also learned how to speak fluently during debates.
